## ‚öôÔ∏è Features & Functionality

### How does missed swipe detection work?
The extension automatically detects days where:
- You're marked "Present" but have less than 1 minute of recorded hours
- Check-in or check-out punches are missing

[Learn more about Missed Swipe Detection](Missed-Swipe-Detection.md)

### What counts as "Incomplete Hours"?
Any working day with less than 8 hours of work is flagged as incomplete (excluding breaks).

### Can I customize the work hours requirement?
Yes! Go to Settings and adjust:
- Shift hours (default: 8 hours)
- Break requirement (default: 1 hour)
- Grace period for late arrivals (default: 5 minutes)

### How accurate is the time tracking?
The extension reads data directly from Keka, so it's as accurate as your Keka attendance records.

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Extension shows no data
**Solution**:
1. Make sure you're on the Keka attendance page
2. Refresh the page
3. Click the extension icon again
4. Check that you're logged into Keka

### Times are incorrect
**Solution**:
1. Verify your Keka data is correct
2. Check your timezone settings
3. Refresh the extension

### Export button doesn't work
**Solution**:
1. Ensure you have data loaded
2. Try refreshing the extension
3. Check browser console for errors

[Full Troubleshooting Guide](Troubleshooting.md)

### Extension keeps crashing
**Solution**:
1. Update to the latest version
2. Clear browser cache
3. Reinstall the extension
4. Report the issue on GitHub
